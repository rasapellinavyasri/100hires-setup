## Video 1

**Title:** Build a One-Person Business

**Link:** https://www.youtube.com/@JustinWelsh

### Key Takeaways

- Focus on building a personal brand before selling products.
- Consistency is more important than perfection.
- Create content that solves one problem at a time.
- Build multiple income streams from your audience.
- Simplicity and repetition help grow an online business.

## Video 2

**Title:** LinkedIn Content Strategy

**Link:** https://www.youtube.com/@JustinWelsh

### Key Takeaways

- Write from personal experience instead of copying others.
- Start posts with a strong hook to grab attention.
- Keep content simple and easy to read.
- Publish consistently to build trust.
- Focus on providing value rather than selling.

---

## Overall Learnings

- Personal branding is a long-term asset.
- Consistency creates audience trust.
- Educational content performs better than promotional content.
- Simple writing increases engagement.
- A one-person business can scale through systems and digital products.
